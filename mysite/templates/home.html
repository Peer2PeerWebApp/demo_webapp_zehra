Why We Are Here

In modern day academia, students are often found in desperate need of help in certain subjects. Even with the help of TA/professor office hours, this sometimes is simply not enough. On the flip side, some students may be found in a financial struggle when in university and have trouble coming across any jobs that can match their busy schedules. Our motivation is to help solve both of these issues. Our project Peer-2-Peer is an online tutoring platform “for students by students”. Our mission aims to create a more simplistic and idealistic platform for students of all universities.
